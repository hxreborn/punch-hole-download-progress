<p>Xposed module that renders download progress as an animated ring around the camera cutout.</p>

<p>System download notifications are intercepted and visualized with customizable colors, animations, and haptic feedback. Features include an active download counter and battery saver-aware rendering.</p>

<p>Requires Android 9+, LSPosed, and a Pixel/AOSP-based ROM (other ROMs may not work as expected).</p>
