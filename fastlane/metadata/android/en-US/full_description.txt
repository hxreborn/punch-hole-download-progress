<p>Xposed module that renders download progress as an animated ring around the camera cutout.</p>

<p>Hooks system download notifications and renders them with configurable colors, animations, and haptic feedback. Supports an active download counter, battery saver-aware rendering, per-rotation (0°/90°/180°/270°) calibration for text and badge offsets, and optional vertical filename text in landscape.</p>

<p>Requires Android 9+, LSPosed, and a Pixel/AOSP-based ROM (other ROMs may not work as expected).</p>
